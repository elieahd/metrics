Deployment Frequency measures how often code is successfully released to production. Higher frequency reflects smaller, safer, and more iterative delivery cycles, reducing risk and enabling faster feedback loops.
Elite On demand — multiple deployments per day

Low Less than once per month — slow, high-risk release cycle
